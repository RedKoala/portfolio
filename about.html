I am a UX Researcher and Designer in the Human Computer Interaction Group at NASA Ames Research Center. Outside of work, I enjoy scubadiving, tinkering, and kickboxing.

I am passionate about encouraging girls in STEM through mentoring and speaking engagements. I especially enjoyed my time as a summer mentor in the NASA Girls and Boys program, where I introduced middle-school aged girls to coding through graphical programming and to basic circuitry with Arduino.

I'm a fan of Creative Commons. Credit for the icons used throughout this site: Rocket by ProSymbols, Augmented Reality by Bharat, chart by Creative Stall, arduino by uizin, screw by Tomas Knopp, and Chemistry by Strohberto - all from the Noun Project.
